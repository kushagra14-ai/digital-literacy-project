##### Do’s

* Think before you post – Make sure your content reflects your values and won’t harm your reputation.
* Protect your privacy – Adjust settings and avoid sharing personal details like your address or schedule.
* Be respectful and kind – Engage in positive, constructive conversations.
* Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading misinformation by checking reliable sources.
* Build a positive digital footprint – Share achievements, projects, and interests that support your goals.



#### 

#### Don’ts

* Don’t overshare personal information – This can put your safety at risk.
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – It can affect future job or academic opportunities.
* Don’t engage in online arguments or harassment – It can escalate quickly and damage your image.
* Don’t plagiarize or steal content – Always give credit where it’s due.
* Don’t use social media excessively – Balance your time to stay focused on studies and well-being.
